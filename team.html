Our Team

Meet the people behind Seedle. Our team is passionate about making mental health resources accessible and building tools that connect communities with the support they need. We bring together diverse skills in technology, healthcare, and community engagement to create a platform that makes support easy to find and simple to understand. By working collaboratively and with compassion, we are committed to breaking down barriers, reducing stigma, and ensuring that no one feels alone when reaching out for help.
